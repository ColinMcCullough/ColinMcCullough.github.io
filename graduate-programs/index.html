University of Oregon

Software Development

I absolutely love living in Oregon grew up in Eugene. The University of Oregon is considered to be one of the top schools in the state so its an obvious option for myself. Software engineering is the current computer science degree path I am most seriously considering and the UO has an undergrad program specifically tailored for it. This degree would give me the skill set necessary to hopefully advance into the Engineering department at my current workplace as a Junior Software Engineer. UO also has a pretty amazing graduate program designing graphics and animation software that sounds incredibly interesting.

Oregon State University

Web and Mobile Software Development

Being able to complete my undergrad while living in Bend at OSU Cascades makes the program incredibly desirable to me. I have heard nothing but great things about the Web and Mobile Software Development program Yong is running. I have some friends who are currently enrolled in the program in Bend as Juniors and Seniors and have already been successful finding work in the industry within Bend. I am really passionate about coming up with ideas to improve efficiency in all aspects of my life. What I find most fascinating about developing mobile apps is it gives you the freedom to come up with a problem or an inefficiency you may be experiencing and develop a solution that humans could not solve without software.
